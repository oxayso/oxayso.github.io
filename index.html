About Me

Growing up, I've always had a bulging intuition for matters that would easily be missed by others in the crowd. It could either be an elephant in the room, or a certain way an individual is feeling that could only be read by the expression in their eyes. I would rather tend to the thousand and one details that need to be accomplished to keep any group going. Usually quite unselfish, I will toil long hours in the service of any worthy cause that demands my attention. But I need to be careful that my perfectionist tendencies don't get in the way of making real progress. In this day and age, speaking from the heart has seem to become a rarity; something that is only found in people who are perceived to be "in touch" with themselves. It isn't necessarily an issue for one to be blamed, rather, we must aid in the progression of one another to open up to our desirable "true" selves. Additionally, to open the doors that will allow us to express our emotions on a level of raw honesty.
